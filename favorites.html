A Quote That Represents Me

“Do not allow people to dim your shine because they are blinded. Tell them to put on some sunglasses.” - Lady Gaga

Lady Gaga’s quote represents me because I have spent too much time wondering whether my ambition, emotion, creativity, and intensity are “too much” for other people. But the quote reminds me that my light is not something I need to reduce just because someone else is uncomfortable standing near it. The sunglasses symbolize the world learning to adjust to my brightness instead of me constantly dimming myself to fit into smaller spaces. I am allowed to be intelligent, dramatic, passionate, artistic, and driven all at once. I was not born to be watered down ... I was born to shine fully.
