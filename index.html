About Me

Hi, my name is Ayman Rahman. I am an incoming UC San Diego Biology (Bioinformatics) student, and this website is a small introduction to who I am, what I care about, and where I hope to go in the future.

I am someone who lives between two worlds: the precision of science and the emotion of music. I love learning about biology, genetics, and bioinformatics, but I also love the expressive power of violin and orchestra. Both sides of me shape the way I see the world.

Through this website, you will learn about my hobbies, music interests, future plans, and some of my favorite things.
