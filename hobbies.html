What I Like to Do

My hobbies show the different sides of my personality. Some of them are creative, some are academic, and some are just ways for me to relax and enjoy life.

  • Playing violin and performing in orchestra
  • Listening to music
  • Learning about biology, genetics, and bioinformatics
  • Writing and reflecting on life
  • Creating ideas for art, music, and future projects

Why These Hobbies Matter

Violin gives me a way to express emotions that are difficult to explain with words. Science gives me a way to understand the world with evidence, patterns, and logic. Together, these hobbies make me feel like I am building both my mind and my identity.
